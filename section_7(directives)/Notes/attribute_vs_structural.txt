Attribute Directives
- You never destroy element from dom, only can do things like change color.
- Look like normal HTML Attribute(possibly with databinding or event binding)
- Only affect/change the element they are added to.
Structural Directives
- Change structure of the dom arround it.
- Look like a normal HTML Attribute but have a leading *(for desugaring)
- Affect a whole area in the DOM (elements get added/remove)
- the * indicates that it is Structural
